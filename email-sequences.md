## OBJECTION HANDLING QUICK REFERENCE

### "We don't want to be salesy"

**Response:**
"This isn't about selling. It's about connection. Think of it 
like a church directory, but for work. Your members choose to 
list their businesses. They choose who to contact. No one is 
marketed to without opting in."

---

### "Our members don't like marketing"

**Response:**
"Neither do ours. That's why we don't call it marketing. We 
call it stewardship. Your members are already in business. 
We're just helping them do it together."

---

### "We don't have budget"

**Response:**
"The free tier is exactly that — free. Start there. Upgrade 
when you see value. Many churches run on the free tier 
indefinitely."

---

### "How do we know members will use it?"

**Response:**
"You don't. But you also don't know if they'll use your small 
groups until you offer them. The cost of trying is zero. And 
we give you a complete launch kit to maximize adoption."

---

### "This feels like a business, not ministry"

**Response:**
"Paul made tents. Lydia sold purple. Work IS ministry when 
it's done with excellence and integrity. We're just helping 
your members see that. And pastors who use this tell us it's 
one of their most effective pastoral care tools."

---

### "We need to pray about it"

**Response:**
"Absolutely. I'll follow up next week. Meanwhile, I'll send 
you a sample directory from a similar church so you can see 
what's possible."

## 30-MINUTE ZOOM CALL STRUCTURE

### Minutes 0-5: Connection
- Thank them for time
- Ask: "What do you know about your business owners' work lives?"
- Listen for pain points (isolation, lack of connection)

### Minutes 5-15: The Demo
- Share screen, show sample directory
- "This is what it looks like for your members"
- Walk through: listing creation, search, connection

### Minutes 15-20: The Models
- Present 3 options (Free / Investment / Sponsor)
- "Most churches start free. Some invest in their members. 
  A few find sponsors. What feels right for you?"

### Minutes 20-25: Objection Handling
- Address concerns (see scripts above)
- "What questions do you have?"

### Minutes 25-30: Next Steps
- If interested: "Let's get your directory live. I need [list]."
- If hesitant: "What would need to be true for this to work?"
- Schedule follow-up or close
