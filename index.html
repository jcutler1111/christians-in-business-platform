Frequently Asked Questions

"Is this just a way to sell to our members?"

No. This is about connection, not extraction. Think of it like a church directory, but for work. Members choose to list their businesses. They choose who to contact. No one is marketed to without opting in.

"What if we don't have many business owners?"

You'd be surprised. Most churches have 15-20% of members who own businesses or are self-employed. A 500-person church typically has 75+ business owners. Plus professionals (lawyers, doctors, consultants) who also benefit.

"How is this different from Facebook or LinkedIn?"

Those are public platforms with algorithms, ads, and noise. This is private, church-only, and focused. Your members aren't competing with the world. They're supporting each other.

"What if we start free and want to upgrade later?"

Easy. You can invest in your members anytime. We'll prorate based on current listings. Many churches start free, see the value, then upgrade within 90 days.

"Who owns the data?"

You do. The church owns the directory. Members own their listings. We just provide the platform. If you ever leave, you can export everything.

"How do we get members to actually use it?"

We give you a complete launch kit: email templates, Sunday slides, social posts, and a 30-day rollout plan. Churches that follow the plan see 60%+ adoption in the first month.
